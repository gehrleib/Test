From what I understand, there are two main issues, both stemming from how RegFlow and RegInsight connect to our RegGroups.

After completing RegFlow or RegInsight, users in RegComp must reassess whether the RegGroup applies to or imposes requirements on RBC and apply a regulatory theme or update the theme if needed. This is because RegFlow and RegInsight operate at a higher level, whereas RegComp focuses specifically on RegGroups. Regulatory theme changes are never determined in CUBE. A theme is only required once a RegGroup is confirmed to both apply to and impose requirements on RBC. While this process originates from Norton Rose, this level of detail is likely still necessary.
